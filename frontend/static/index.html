    Water Quality Level vs NH3-N

    Classification rule used by the realtime water quality box.

    Water Class NH3-N (mg/L) Description
    Class I ≤ 0.15 Pristine; source of drinking water and nature reserves.
    Class II ≤ 0.5 Clean; primary protection zones for drinking water.
    Class III ≤ 1.0 Fair; secondary protection zones and swimming areas.
    Class IV ≤ 1.5 Poor; industrial water supply and general recreation.
    Class V ≤ 2.0 Very poor; agricultural use and general landscape.
    > Class V > 2.0 Black-odorous water; essentially lacks functional use.
